My Books

Here you can find out more information about the various books I'm working on. I have a number of book series from duologies and trilogies to one seven-book series(!), as well as many standalone books that I’m either writing or planning or thinking about.

Most of my books fall somewhere in the genres of sci-fi, fantasy and M/M romance, with audiences mostly in the young adult or new adult area. But subgenres, themes and settings range wildly, so there should be something for everyone!
